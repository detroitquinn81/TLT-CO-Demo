Our Story

Born in Towne Lake.
Built for Woodstock.

We started Towne Lake Taco Co. because Woodstock deserved something real — not a chain, not a franchise. Tacos made with care, using fresh local produce and family recipes that have been in our kitchen for generations.

Whether you're grabbing lunch between meetings on Towne Lake Parkway, hitting the lake after work, or settling in for taco night with the family — we've got you.
